Hi, my name's Aaron Powell and I'm a [Cloud Developer Advocate at Microsoft](https://developer.microsoft.com/advocates?{{<cda>}}), based in Sydney, Australia. I've been at Microsoft since 2019, working with developer communities around AI tooling, .NET, and the web.

These days my work sits firmly at the intersection of AI and developer productivity. I spend most of my time advocating for, and contributing to, projects that help developers get the most out of AI-assisted development.

## What I'm Working On Now

### [Awesome Copilot](https://awesome-copilot.github.com/)

A community-curated list of GitHub Copilot extensions, instructions, prompts, and resources. What started as a small experiment has grown to over 20k GitHub stars and 500+ resources — it's pretty much my full-time focus right now, managing the OSS project and collaborating with product teams to make it even better.

→ **[Explore Awesome Copilot](https://awesome-copilot.github.com/)**

### [Aspire Community Toolkit](https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Aspire)

A community-driven collection of integrations and components for .NET Aspire. I'm one of the maintainers and it's been incredible to watch it grow to nearly 40 integrations, with some of that work finding its way into Aspire itself.
